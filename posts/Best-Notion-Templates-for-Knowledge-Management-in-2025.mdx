## How to Choose the Right Template

Consider:
1. **Your workflow** - Research vs creation vs learning
2. **Complexity tolerance** - Simple vs comprehensive
3. **Customization needs** - Rigid vs flexible
4. **Team vs personal** - Collaboration requirements

## Common Template Mistakes

**Mistake 1: Over-complication**
Don't use all properties. Start minimal, add as needed.

**Mistake 2: Template hoarding**
Pick ONE system, commit for 30 days before switching.

**Mistake 3: No adaptation**
Templates are starting points, not final solutions.
